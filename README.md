<h1>Construction, Sales & Safety Suite</h1>

<h2>Project Overview</h2>
This project is a responive web application designed to streamline the workflow between site safety compliance, equipment rental management, and the sales estimation process.


<h2>The "Why" Behind This Project</h2>
I chose to build this specific suite because of my background in Construction, Sales, and Health & Safety (H&S). While I do not claim to be an expert in these fields, my hands-on experience gave me a front-row seat to the common communication gaps that happen between the office and the field.


I am using my industry knowledge to ensure this tool solves real-world problems:  
Safety: Prioritizing high-visibility "Safety First" layouts for mobile-using foremen.  
Sales: Integrating quick calculation tools to help reps provide faster, more accurate quotes.  
Construction: Organizing inventory in a way that reflects actual on-site equipment tracking needs.
